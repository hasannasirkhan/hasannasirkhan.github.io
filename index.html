About me

♦ I am currently a Research Associate at the National Center of Artificial Intelligence (NCAI), Pakistan, concentrating on computer vision and machine learning.

♦ In 2019, I joined NCAI and leads a team of six fellows to develop an AI-based DICOM viewer for breast cancer diagnosis called “Tashkhees”. I work closely with software developers, radiologists, and pathologists, to develop algorithms for solving clinical problems.

♦ On the research side, I am working on ✔ homogeneous and heterogeneous transfer learning ✔ weakly-supervised learning, vision transformers ✔ generative adversarial networks ✔ feature fusion in 2D and 3D medical imaging

♦ I have developed/applied AI and ML algorithms to various applications, including but not limited to ✔ Breast cancer diagnosis using radiology images (mammogram) ✔ Computer-aided diagnostic tool for gigapixel whole-slide histopathology images ✔ Precision agriculture using high-resolution satellite images

✪ Skills: TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, NumPy, OpenCV, Matplotlib, OpenMMLab etc.
